CC   -!- DISRUPTION PHENOTYPE: Cells lacking this gene accumulate
CC       coproporphyrin-III only under anaerobic conditions, however no
CC       obvious respiratory growth phenotype is observed under anaerobic
CC       conditions. The hemN hemZ double mutant do not abolish aerobic and
CC       anaerobic respiratory growth, however a reduction of growth is
CC       observed. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:10498703}.
CC   -!- MISCELLANEOUS: HemZ is able to complement a S.typhimurium hemF
CC       hemN double mutant under aerobic and anaerobic conditions, however
CC       under anaerobic growth conditions, the double mutant complemented
CC       with hemZ grows significantly faster.
CC       {ECO:0000305|PubMed:10498703}.
CC   -!- SIMILARITY: Belongs to the anaerobic coproporphyrinogen-III
CC       oxidase family. HemZ subfamily. {ECO:0000305}.
CC   -!- CAUTION: Although the enzyme shows to be able to complement hemF
CC       hemN double mutant and to accumulate coproporphyrinogen-III when
CC       the gene is disrupted, no oxygen-independent coproporphyrinogen-
CC       III oxidase activity has been shown. The exact role of this
CC       protein is still unknown. {ECO:0000305}.
