CC   -!- FUNCTION: Involved in the biosynthesis of methylglucose
CC       lipopolysaccharides (MGLPs). Catalyzes the condensation of NDP-
CC       glucose and 3-phospho-glycerate (3-PGA) to yield glucosyl-3-
CC       phosphoglycerate (GPG). It can use UDP-glucose, ADP-glucose and
CC       GDP-glucose as donor substrates with decreasing affinity, and 3-
CC       PGA as an acceptor. D-glycerate could only be the acceptor with
CC       ADP-glucose and at a very low rate. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:18221489}.
CC   -!- CATALYTIC ACTIVITY: NDP-glucose + 3-phospho-D-glycerate = NDP + 2-
CC       O-(alpha-D-glucopyranosyl)-3-phospho-D-glycerate.
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:18221489}.
