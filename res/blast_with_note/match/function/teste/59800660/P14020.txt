CC       is the mannosyl donor in pathways leading to N-glycosylation,
CC       glycosyl phosphatidylinositol membrane anchoring, and O-
CC       mannosylation of proteins.
CC   -!- CATALYTIC ACTIVITY: GDP-mannose + dolichyl phosphate = GDP +
CC       dolichyl D-mannosyl phosphate.

CC         KM=1.2 uM for GDP-mannose {ECO:0000269|PubMed:15548536};
CC         Vmax=25.1 nmol/min/mg enzyme for GDP-mannose
CC         {ECO:0000269|PubMed:15548536};
CC         Note=For the phosphorylated protein, the Vmax increases to 146.7
CC         nmol/min/mg enzyme, whereas the KM stays at 1.1 uM for GDP-
CC         mannose, increasing the catalytic efficiency 6-fold.;
