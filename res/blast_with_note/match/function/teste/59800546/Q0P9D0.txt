CC       step in the biosynthesis of the undecaprenyl-linked
CC       heptasaccharide donor in the N-linked protein glycosylation
CC       pathway. Catalyzes the linking of uridine 5'-
CC       diphosphobacillosamine (UDP-Bac) to undecaprenyl phosphate to
CC       create the first membrane-associated intermediate
CC       undecaprenylpyrophosphate-linked Bac (Und-PP-Bac).

CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:16618123}.
