CC   -!- FUNCTION: Endoribonuclease that plays a central role in RNA
CC       processing and decay. Required for the maturation of 5S and 16S
CC       rRNAs and the majority of tRNAs. Also involved in the degradation
CC       of most mRNAs. Can also process other RNA species, such as RNAI, a
CC       molecule that controls the replication of ColE1 plasmid, and the
CC       cell division inhibitor DicF-RNA. It initiates the decay of RNAs
CC       by cutting them internally near their 5'-end. It is able to remove
CC       poly(A) tails by an endonucleolytic process. {ECO:0000255|HAMAP-
