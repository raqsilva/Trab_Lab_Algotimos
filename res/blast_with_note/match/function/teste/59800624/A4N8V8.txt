CC       binding component (Potential). Acts as an adhesin that promotes
CC       binding of H.influenzae to host laminin and vitronectin. In

CC       role in bacterial serum resistance. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:23230060,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:23387957, ECO:0000305}.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Interacts with host laminin and vitronectin. Can interact
