CC   -!- FUNCTION: Member of the two-component regulatory system RssA/RssB
CC       involved in regulation of swarming motility which has been shown
CC       to be inhibited by saturated fatty acids. RssA/RssB regulates
CC       cellular fatty acid composition, hemolysin production and cell
CC       surface topography. RssA/RssB negatively regulates the activity of
CC       SlhBA. It can also act as a negative regulator for the control of
CC       the swarming initiation. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:15866926}.
CC   -!- CATALYTIC ACTIVITY: ATP + protein L-histidine = ADP + protein N-
CC       phospho-L-histidine.
CC   -!- SUBCELLULAR LOCATION: Cell inner membrane {ECO:0000305}; Multi-
CC       pass membrane protein {ECO:0000305}.
CC   -!- DISRUPTION PHENOTYPE: Mutation in either rssA or rssB confers a
CC       precocious-swarming phenotype on LB agar: swarming occurs at 37
CC       degrees Celsius and is initiated at a lower cell density and more
CC       rapidly than the swarming of the parent strain at 30 degrees
CC       Celsius. Both mutants also exhibit increased hemolysin activity
CC       and altered cell surface topology. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:16077114}.
CC   -!- SIMILARITY: Contains 1 histidine kinase domain.
CC       {ECO:0000255|PROSITE-ProRule:PRU00107}.
