CC   -!- FUNCTION: Functions as an ammonium and methylammonium transporter
CC       in the absence of glutamine. Required for ammonium utilization at
CC       low concentrations or at low pH values, when ammonium is the
CC       single nitrogen source. Required for binding of NrgB to the
CC       membrane. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:14600241}.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Interacts with NrgB for a correct localization of the
CC       latter.
CC   -!- SUBCELLULAR LOCATION: Cell membrane {ECO:0000305}; Multi-pass
CC       membrane protein {ECO:0000305}.
CC   -!- INDUCTION: The nrgAB operon is activated by TnrA and NrgB is
CC       required for full induction of the operon under conditions of
CC       ammonium limitation. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:12823818}.
CC   -!- DISRUPTION PHENOTYPE: Cells grow poorly at pH 5.5 and not at all
CC       at pH 5.0 with ammonium as their sole nitrogen source. They are
CC       unable to transport the ammonium analog methylammonium, and thus
CC       probably also ammonium. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:14600241}.
CC   -!- SIMILARITY: Belongs to the ammonia transporter channel
CC       (TC 1.A.11.2) family. {ECO:0000305}.
