CC   -!- FUNCTION: C-type cytochrome. Part of the cbb3-type cytochrome c
CC       oxidase complex. CcoP subunit is required for transferring
CC       electrons from donor cytochrome c via its heme groups to CcoO
CC       subunit. From there, electrons are shuttled to the catalytic
CC       binuclear center of CcoN subunit where oxygen reduction takes
CC       place. The complex also functions as a proton pump (By
CC       similarity). {ECO:0000250|UniProtKB:D5ARP7,
