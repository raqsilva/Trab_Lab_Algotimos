CC   -!- FUNCTION: Is a secondary, electrogenic Na(+)/H(+) antiporter that
CC       catalyzes Na(+) uptake and proton efflux. Makes modest
CC       contributions to pH homeostasis in the alkaline range of pH but is
CC       not contributor to Na(+) resistance. Appears to have a repressive
CC       effect on growth and on alkaline phosphatases production in the
CC       presence of sodium, by affecting the transcription of the
CC       phoP/phoR two-component regulatory system.
CC   -!- ENZYME REGULATION: The antiport activity is markedly inhibited by
CC       both valinomycin and CCCP, and modestly by nigericin.
CC   -!- SUBCELLULAR LOCATION: Cell membrane; Multi-pass membrane protein.
CC   -!- SIMILARITY: Belongs to the NhaC Na(+)/H(+) (TC 2.A.35) antiporter
CC       family. {ECO:0000305}.
