CC       on adenosine at position 37 (t(6)A37) in tRNAs that read codons

CC       threonylcarbamoyl moiety of threonylcarbamoyl-AMP (TC-AMP) to the
CC       N6 group of A37, together with TsaD and TsaE; this reaction does
CC       not require ATP in vitro. TsaB seems to play an indirect role in
CC       the t(6)A biosynthesis pathway, possibly in regulating the core
