CC       RNA-dependent ATPase activity and unwinds double-stranded RNA. May
CC       play a role in the interconversion of ribosomal RNA-folding

CC       ribosome maturation. {ECO:0000255|HAMAP-Rule:MF_00968,

CC   -!- CATALYTIC ACTIVITY: ATP + H(2)O = ADP + phosphate.
CC       {ECO:0000255|HAMAP-Rule:MF_00968, ECO:0000269|PubMed:15196029}.
CC   -!- ENZYME REGULATION: ATPase activity is stimulated by both long RNAs
CC       and short oligoribonucleotides. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:15196029}.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Interacts with PcnB. Interacts in vitro with RNase E.
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:10361280, ECO:0000269|PubMed:15554979}.
CC   -!- SUBCELLULAR LOCATION: Cytoplasm {ECO:0000255|HAMAP-Rule:MF_00968,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:18083833}. Note=Ribosome-associated. Can either
CC       bind to the intact 70S ribosome or to individual ribosomal
CC       subunits.
