CC   -!- FUNCTION: Subunit of the protein translocation channel SecYEG.
CC       Overexpression of some hybrid proteins has been thought to jam the
CC       protein secretion apparatus resulting in cell death; while this
CC       may be true it also results in FtsH-mediated degradation of SecY.
CC       Treatment with antibiotics that block translation elongation such
CC       as chloramphenicol also leads to degradation of SecY and SecE but
CC       not SecG.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Component of the Sec protein translocase complex.
CC       Heterotrimer consisting of SecY, SecE and SecG subunits. The
CC       heterotrimers can form oligomers, although 1 heterotrimer is
CC       thought to be able to translocate proteins. SecG probably contacts
CC       ribosomal protein L23 and/or L29 when the translocation complex is
CC       docked with the ribosome.
