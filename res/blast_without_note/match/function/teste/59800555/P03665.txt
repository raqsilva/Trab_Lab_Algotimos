CC   -!- FUNCTION: Acts in the assembly and extrusion of the bacteriophage
CC       by forming a channel across the host outer membrane. This channel
CC       is just large enough to allow a newly synthesized phage particle
CC       to pass through. Extrusion is a process of concomitant assembly
CC       and secretion and takes place at specific assembly sites where
CC       host inner and outer membranes are in close contacts (By
CC       similarity). {ECO:0000250}.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Homomultimer. The channel is composed of 14 G4P subunits
CC       that confer a barrel-like structure. Interacts with G1P; this
CC       interaction results in a complex that spans the inner an outer
CC       host membranes (By similarity). {ECO:0000250}.
