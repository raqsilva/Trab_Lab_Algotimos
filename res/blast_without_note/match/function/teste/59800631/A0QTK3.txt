CC   -!- FUNCTION: Member of the two-component regulatory system MtrA/MtrB.
CC       Probably functions as a membrane-associated protein kinase that
CC       phosphorylates MtrA in response to environmental signals (By
CC       similarity). MtrB may be active only at septa where it promotes
CC       MtrA phosphorylation and MtrA regulon expression, probably playing
CC       a role in cell division. {ECO:0000250,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:22610443}.
CC   -!- CATALYTIC ACTIVITY: ATP + protein L-histidine = ADP + protein N-
CC       phospho-L-histidine.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Interacts with MrtA. {ECO:0000305}.
CC   -!- SUBCELLULAR LOCATION: Cell membrane {ECO:0000269|PubMed:22610443};
CC       Multi-pass membrane protein {ECO:0000269|PubMed:22610443}.
CC       Note=Found at the septa or mid-cell; correct localization requires
CC       correct FtsZ localization.
CC   -!- DISRUPTION PHENOTYPE: Grows poorly with filamentous cells, often
CC       with bulbous regions and a chain-like phenotype indicating
CC       possible defects in septum splitting and cell wall metabolism.
CC       Increased sensitivity to lysozyme. Decreased expression of the
CC       MtrA/MtrB regulon genes dnaA, ripA, and fbpB.
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:22610443}.
