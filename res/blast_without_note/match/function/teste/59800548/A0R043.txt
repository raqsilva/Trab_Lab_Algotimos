CC   -!- FUNCTION: Catalyzes the addition of a mannose residue from GDP-D-
CC       mannose to the position 6 of a phosphatidyl-myo-inositol bearing
CC       an alpha-1,2-linked mannose residue (PIM1) to generate
CC       phosphatidyl-myo-inositol bearing alpha-1,2- and alpha-1,6-linked
CC       mannose residues (PIM2). Can also add a mannose residue to the
CC       position 2 of a phosphatidyl-myo-inositol (PI) to generate a
CC       phosphatidyl-myo-inositol mannoside (PIM1) or to the position 6 of
CC       a monoacyl phosphatidyl-myo-inositol bearing an alpha-1,2-linked

CC       inositol dimannoside (AcPIM2). {ECO:0000269|PubMed:19638342}.
