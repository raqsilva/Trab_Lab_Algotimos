CC   -!- FUNCTION: Member of the two-component regulatory system CopS/CopR.
CC       Involved in the activation of copper resistance gene operon
CC       CopABCD. Phosphorylation of CopR by CopS would convert it into an
CC       active state to induce expression of the cop operon by binding to
CC       a specific site on the cop operon promoter (cop box) and possibly
CC       by facilitating the binding of RNA polymerase to the cop promoter.
CC       CopR also binds to the chromosomally encoded cop operon promoter.
CC       May also be involved in basic copper metabolism.
