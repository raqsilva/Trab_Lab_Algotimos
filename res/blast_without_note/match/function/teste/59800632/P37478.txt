CC   -!- FUNCTION: Member of the two-component regulatory system YycG/YycF
CC       involved in the regulation of the ftsAZ operon, the yocH, ykvT,
CC       cwlO, lytE, ydjM, yjeA, yoeB genes and the tagAB and tagDEF
CC       operons. Probably phosphorylates YycF. Binds to the ftsAZ P1
CC       promoter sequence in vitro. YycF has been shown to directly bind
CC       to the regulatory regions of yocH, ykvT, tagAB/tagDEF. Activates
CC       cwlO, lytE and ydjM and represses yoeB and yjeA.
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:10878122, ECO:0000269|PubMed:17581128,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:9829949}.
