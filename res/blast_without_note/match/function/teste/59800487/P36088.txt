CC   -!- DISRUPTION PHENOTYPE: Single deletion of fRMsr has complete growth
CC       inhibition on methionine-R-sulfoxide medium and fRMsr and MXR1
CC       double deletion completely blocks the growth on both methionine-R-
CC       sulfoxide and methionine-S-sulfoxide medium. FRMsr and MXR2 double
CC       deletion has no effect on growth on methionine-S-sulfoxide medium.
CC       Single mutant or any of the double mutants show no growth defects
CC       in methionine medium, even the fRMsr, MXR1 and MXR2 triple
CC       deletion mutant is viable and grows similarly to wild-type. Single
CC       deletion of fRMsr has an increased sensitivity to oxidative stress
CC       and a decreased life span of 18% compared to wild-type. FRMsr and
CC       MXR1, as well as fRMsr and MXR2 double mutants, and fRMsr, MXR1
CC       and MXR2 triple mutant show 20% reduction in life span compared
CC       with wild-type cells. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:19049972}.
