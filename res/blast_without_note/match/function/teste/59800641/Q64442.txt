CC       a role in the sperm motility by providing an energetic source for
CC       sperm. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:18799757}.

CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:18799757}; Peripheral membrane protein
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:18799757}. Cell projection, cilium, flagellum
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:18799757}. Note=Associated with mitochondria
CC       of the midpiece and near the plasma membrane in the principal
CC       piece of the flagellum. Also found in the epididymosome, secreted
CC       by the epididymal epithelium and that transfers proteins from the
CC       epididymal fluid to the sperm surface.
CC   -!- TISSUE SPECIFICITY: Testis has the highest level of expression,

CC       also observed in lens, brain, and skeletal muscle. Expressed in

CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:18799757, ECO:0000269|PubMed:7601136}.
