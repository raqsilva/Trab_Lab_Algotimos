CC   -!- FUNCTION: Regulatory protein involved in autolytic activity,
CC       multidrug resistance and virulence. Controls autolysis by
CC       inactivating LytM, LytN (autolysins) and SarV (autolysis
CC       activator) and activating ArlRS, LrgAB and LytSR (autolysis
CC       inhibitors). Acts as a dual regulator for resistance to multiple
CC       drugs by inactivating NorB and tet38 and activating NorA.
CC       Positively controls the expression of virulence accessory gene
CC       regulator (agr) to promote alpha-hemolysin (hla) transcription and
CC       down-regulates staphylococcal accessory regulator (sarS), leading
CC       to repression of surface protein A (spa). Binds directly to hla
CC       promoter to augment its activation. Binds to sarS promoter to
CC       down-regulate spa expression. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:12730173,
