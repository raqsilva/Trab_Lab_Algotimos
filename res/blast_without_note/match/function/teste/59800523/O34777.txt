CC   -!- FUNCTION: Organic peroxide sensor. Represses the expression of the
CC       peroxide-inducible gene ohrA by cooperative binding to two
CC       inverted repeat elements. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:11418552}.
CC   -!- ENZYME REGULATION: Inactivated by oxidation of Cys-15 to a
CC       sulfenic acid.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Homodimer.
CC   -!- SUBCELLULAR LOCATION: Cytoplasm.
CC   -!- PTM: Cys-15 is oxidized by organic peroxides to cysteine sulfenic
CC       acid (Cys-SOH). This can react with the alpha-amido of the
CC       following residue to form the sulfenamide cross-link. Oxidation or
CC       cross-linking results in the loss of DNA-binding activity and the
CC       inactivation of repressor function. Both the cysteine sulfenic
CC       acid and the sulfenamide cross-link can react with free cysteine
CC       or bacillithiol to form mixed disulfides. Further reduction of
CC       OhrR by free sulfhydryl compounds restores repressor activity.
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:11983871}.
