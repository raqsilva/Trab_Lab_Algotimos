CC   -!- DISRUPTION PHENOTYPE: Defects in corneal development. While eyes
CC       contain all anterior and posterior segment components, corneas
CC       show developmental abnormalities characterized by decreased
CC       cellularity of the apical epithelial layer and disorganized basal
CC       and wing cell layers. Defects are due to depletion of quiescent
CC       limbal stem cells, leading to enhanced proliferation and
CC       apoptosis, and resulting in defective corneal differentiation and
CC       wound healing. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:25030174}.
