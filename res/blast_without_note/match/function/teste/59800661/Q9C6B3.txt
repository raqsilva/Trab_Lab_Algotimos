CC       does not mediates the reversible hydration of carbon dioxide

CC       respiration. Binds HCO(3)-. Required for male fertility during
CC       anther development and dehiscence to regulate the secondary
CC       thickenings of the endothecial cell wall, probably by modulating
CC       H(2)O(2)-dependent lignin polymerization.
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:15935378, ECO:0000269|PubMed:19326245,
