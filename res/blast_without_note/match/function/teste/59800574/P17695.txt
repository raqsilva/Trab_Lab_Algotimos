CC   -!- FUNCTION: Component of the glutathione system which performs
CC       several activities such as glutathione-dependent oxidoreductase,
CC       glutathione peroxidase and glutathione S-transferase (GST)
CC       activity. The disulfide bond functions as an electron carrier in
CC       the glutathione-dependent synthesis of deoxyribonucleotides by the
CC       enzyme ribonucleotide reductase. In addition, it is also involved
CC       in reducing cytosolic protein- and non-protein-disulfides in a
CC       coupled system with glutathione reductase. Required for resistance
CC       to reactive oxygen species (ROS) by directly reducing
CC       hydroperoxides and for the detoxification of ROS-mediated damage.
CC       GRX2 is more active as an oxidoreductase than GRX1.
