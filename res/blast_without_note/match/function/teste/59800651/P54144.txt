CC   -!- FUNCTION: High affinity ammonium transporter probably involved in
CC       ammonium uptake from the soil, long-distance transport to the
CC       shoots and re-uptake of apoplastic ammonium that derives from
CC       photorespiration in shoots. Contributes with AMT1-3 to the overall
CC       ammonium uptake capacity in roots under nitrogen-deficiency
CC       conditions. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:12481062,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:16806203, ECO:0000269|PubMed:16917981,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:17026539}.
CC   -!- BIOPHYSICOCHEMICAL PROPERTIES:

CC         KM=22 uM for ammonium chloride (at external pH 6.1)
CC         {ECO:0000269|PubMed:12481062, ECO:0000269|PubMed:16806203};
CC         Note=Measured in yeast knockout mutant YCW012.;
CC   -!- SUBCELLULAR LOCATION: Cell membrane {ECO:0000269|PubMed:15308754,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:16917981, ECO:0000269|PubMed:17026539}; Multi-
CC       pass membrane protein {ECO:0000269|PubMed:15308754,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:16917981, ECO:0000269|PubMed:17026539}.
CC   -!- TISSUE SPECIFICITY: Highly expressed in roots. Expressed in root
CC       tips, root hairs, root epidermis, rhizodermis, cortex and
CC       pericycle. Expressed in leaves epidermal and mesophyll cells.
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:10330477, ECO:0000269|PubMed:16917981,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:17026539, ECO:0000269|PubMed:17085512}.
CC   -!- INDUCTION: By nitrogen deprivation in roots and nitrogen supply in
CC       leaves. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:16917981, ECO:0000269|PubMed:17026539,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:17085512, ECO:0000269|PubMed:17172286}.
CC   -!- DISRUPTION PHENOTYPE: No effect on ammonium uptake. Higher
CC       expression of AMT1-2; AMT1-3 and AMT2-1.
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:12427993}.
CC   -!- SIMILARITY: Belongs to the ammonia transporter channel
CC       (TC 1.A.11.2) family. {ECO:0000305}.
