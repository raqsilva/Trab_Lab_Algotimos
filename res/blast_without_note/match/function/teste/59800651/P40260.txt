CC       and NH4) to use as a nitrogen source. Can also transport
CC       methylamine. The affinity of MEP1 is about twenty times lower than
CC       that of MEP2. MEP3 has the lowest affinity.
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:11486013, ECO:0000269|PubMed:9234685}.
