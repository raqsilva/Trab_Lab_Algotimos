CC   -!- FUNCTION: Transporter for ammonium (both charged and uncharged NH3
CC       and NH4) to use as a nitrogen source. The affinity of MEP2 is
CC       about twenty times higher than that of MEP1. MEP3 has the lowest
CC       affinity. Under ammonium limitation acts as an ammonium sensor,
CC       generating a signal that leads to pseudohyphal growth.
CC       {ECO:0000269|PubMed:11069679, ECO:0000269|PubMed:11486013,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:9234685, ECO:0000269|PubMed:9482721}.
CC   -!- SUBCELLULAR LOCATION: Membrane; Multi-pass membrane protein.
CC   -!- SIMILARITY: Belongs to the ammonia transporter channel
CC       (TC 1.A.11.2) family. {ECO:0000305}.
