CC   -!- FUNCTION: Involved in the GO system responsible for removing an
CC       oxidatively damaged form of guanine (7,8-dihydro-8-oxoguanine, 8-
CC       oxo-dGTP) from DNA and the nucleotide pool. 8-oxo-dGTP is inserted
CC       opposite dA and dC residues of template DNA with almost equal
CC       efficiency thus leading to A.T to G.C transversions (By
CC       similarity). Adenine glycosylase active on G-A mispairs. MutY also
CC       corrects error-prone DNA synthesis past GO lesions which are due
CC       to the oxidatively damaged form of guanine: 7,8-dihydro-8-
CC       oxoguanine (By similarity). {ECO:0000250}.

CC       a monocistronic transcript and a yhfQ-fabL-sspE transcript.

CC       strains show increased mutation rates during exponential and
CC       stationary phase (PubMed:19011023). {ECO:0000269|PubMed:12483591,
