CC   -!- FUNCTION: Proton motive force-driven secondary transporter that
CC       mediates the transport of citrate complexed to Mg(2+).
CC       Cotransports at least two protons per Mg(2+)-citrate complex. Can
CC       also transport citrate in complex with Ni(2+), Mn(2+), Co(2+), and
CC       Zn(2+).
CC   -!- ENZYME REGULATION: The uptake activity increases with increasing
CC       Mg(2+) concentrations. Inhibited by FCCP, TCC and nigericin.
CC   -!- SUBCELLULAR LOCATION: Cell membrane {ECO:0000305}; Multi-pass
CC       membrane protein {ECO:0000305}.
CC   -!- INDUCTION: Expression is under strict control of the medium
CC       composition. Induced by citrate, via the two-component regulatory
CC       system CitT/CitS. Repressed by rapidly metabolized carbon sources
CC       like glucose, glycerol and inositol, via the carbon catabolite
CC       repression system. Is also repressed by succinate and glutamate,
CC       albeit to a lesser extent. {ECO:0000269|PubMed:10972810,
CC       ECO:0000269|PubMed:11029430}.
CC   -!- SIMILARITY: Belongs to the CitM (TC 2.A.11) transporter family.
CC       {ECO:0000305}.
