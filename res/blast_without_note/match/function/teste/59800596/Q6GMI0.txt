CC   -!- FUNCTION: Serine protease with a variety of targets, including
CC       extracellular matrix proteins and proteoglycans. Through cleavage
CC       of proteoglycans, may release soluble FGF-glycosaminoglycan
CC       complexes that promote the range and intensity of FGF signals in
CC       the extracellular space. Regulates the availability of insulin-
CC       like growth factors (IGFs) by cleaving IGF-binding proteins.
CC       Inhibits signaling mediated by TGF-beta family members.
CC       Consequently, may regulate many physiological processes.
CC       Intracellularly, degrades TSC2, leading to the activation of TSC2
CC       downstream targets (By similarity). {ECO:0000250}.
CC   -!- SUBUNIT: Forms homotrimers. In the presence of substrate, may form
CC       higher-order multimers in a PDZ-independent manner. {ECO:0000250}.
CC   -!- SUBCELLULAR LOCATION: Secreted {ECO:0000250}. Cytoplasm, cytosol
CC       {ECO:0000250}. Note=Also found associated with the plasma
CC       membrane. {ECO:0000250}.
